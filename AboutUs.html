About Us

Chi's Shop was founded in 1997 in Atlanta, Georgia. It was founded by three people.

Dr. Lei Li Founder of Chi's Shop

Jamie Jamison Founder of Chi's Shop

Foster Scotland Founder of Chi's Shop

Jamie's family has grown coffee beans for many years. Jamie, Lei, and Foster made a decision to start a coffee business in Atlanta, Ga. After about a year, Chi's Shop was known for their amazing coffee and different flavors. This year, 2022, will be our 25 year Anniversary. We started selling Chi's Fav and Nice Touch which have gone viral all over TikTok and has brought Chi's Shop over 300 new customers. In 2023, We plan on expanding the business to Houston, Texas and Los Angeles, California. We will have two new locations in Georgia before the end of 2022.
